The first of its kind blockchain tracking system
for bikes to increase climate action

Our innovative blockchain tracking system for bikes is the
first of its kind and is designed to promote climate action by
making cycling safer and mor accessible

By using blockchain technology, we can provide an
unparalleled level of security and transparency to cyclists,
allowing them to track their bikes in real-time and have peace
of mind knowing their bike is safe from theft. This, in turn,
can encourage more people to take up cycling as a
sustainable mode of transportation,reducing the carbon
footprint of our communities.

Our system also provides law enforcement agencies with a
powerfool tool to combat bike theft,enabling them to quickly
and easily locate stolen bikes and identify the culprits.
